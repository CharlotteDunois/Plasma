PromiseInterface all()

Buffers all rows and returns a promise which resolves with an instance of QueryResultInterface.

This method does not guarantee that all rows get returned, as the buffering depends on when this method gets invoked. There's no automatic buffering, as such rows may be missing if invoked too late.
